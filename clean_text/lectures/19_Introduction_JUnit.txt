Assertions
Assertions are defined in the JUnit class Assert
Ifanassertionistrue,themethodcontinuesexecuting.
Ifanyassertionisfalse,themethodstopsexecutingatthatpoint,and theresultforthetestcasewillbefail.
Ifanyotherexceptionisthrownduringthemethod,theresultforthe testcasewillbeerror.
Ifnoassertionswereviolatedfortheentiremethod,thetestcasewill pass.
Allassertionmethodsarestaticmethods.
